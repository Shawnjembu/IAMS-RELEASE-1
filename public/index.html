Process

How the Attachment Cycle Works

Every stage of industrial attachment is tracked in one place — no emails, no spreadsheets, no lost forms.

1

Register on the Platform

Students create an account with their UB student number, programme, skills, and location preference. Organisations register with company details and intern requirements.

2

Organisations Publish Requirements

Companies describe the type of intern they need — required skills, industry sector, and available locations — so the matching engine can find the best candidates.

3

Students Browse & Apply

Students view approved organisations, check skill and location alignment, and submit applications directly through the platform.

4

Coordinator Matches & Confirms

The attachment coordinator runs the automated matching engine (70% skills, 20% location, 10% interests), reviews suggestions, and confirms or overrides placements.

5

Students Submit Weekly Logbooks

During attachment, students upload weekly logbook entries to document their tasks and learning. Supervisors review and approve each entry.

6

Supervisors Evaluate Performance

The industrial supervisor submits a performance report. The university supervisor conducts two site visits and submits separate evaluations for each visit.

7

Final Results Calculated & Published

The coordinator finalises the grade: 40% from the industrial report + 60% from the average of both university visit evaluations. Students are notified immediately.

Capabilities

Key Platform Features

Everything the department needs to run a structured, transparent, and efficient attachment programme.

🔗

Direct Applications

Students apply to organisations directly through the platform. Application history is tracked in one place for all parties.

🎯

Skill-Based Matching

Automated matching uses a weighted formula — 70% skill overlap, 20% location, 10% interests — to surface the best-fit placements.

📋

Placement Management

Coordinators create and manage all placement records, track statuses (pending → active → completed), and assign supervisors.

📓

Weekly Logbooks

Students submit structured weekly entries. Supervisors review, approve or reject each one with inline comments.

📄

Final Report Submission

Students upload their final attachment report. The supervisor reviews and approves it before the coordinator finalises assessment.

⭐

Dual Evaluation System

Industrial supervisors submit a performance report (40% weight). University supervisors submit two visit evaluations (60% combined).

🔔

Deadline Reminders

The system automatically surfaces logbook due dates, report deadlines, and evaluation windows. Coordinators can push reminders system-wide.

📊

Role-Aware Dashboards

Every user sees only what is relevant to their role — personalised stats, pending actions, and quick-access links from a single dashboard.

Why IAMS

What the Platform Replaces

No More Email Chains

All communication, applications, and submissions happen inside the platform. Nothing gets lost in an inbox or misattributed to the wrong student.

No More Paper Forms

Registration, consent, logbooks, reports, and evaluations are all digital. The coordinator sees every submission as soon as it is made.

No More Spreadsheets

Student lists, placement records, and evaluation scores are stored in a structured database. Reporting and grade calculation happen automatically.

Complete Placement Visibility

The coordinator can see every student's placement status, logbook history, and evaluation progress in real time — not at the end of semester.

Structured Evaluation Pipeline

Industrial reports and university visit evaluations feed directly into the final grade calculation. No manual aggregation, no transcription errors.

One Platform for Everyone

Students, organisations, industrial supervisors, university supervisors, and the coordinator all log into the same system with role-appropriate access.
