Process

How the Attachment Cycle Works

Every stage of industrial attachment is tracked in one place — no emails, no spreadsheets, no lost forms.

1

Students and Organisations Register

Students register using their UB student email format, while organisations create a host profile with contact details, skills needed, location and available attachment slots.

2

Students Choose Available Organisations

Students browse organisations with available slots and select the organisation they want for attachment. Full organisations are blocked automatically.

3

Coordinator Monitors and Supports Placement

The coordinator views students, organisations, available slots and placement status, then helps resolve system-level or placement issues where needed.

4

Supervisors Are Invited and Assigned

Organisations invite and assign industrial supervisors. The coordinator invites and assigns university supervisors. Each supervisor can handle up to ten students.

5

Students Submit Logbooks and Reports

Students submit weekly logbooks and final reports after placement. Their assigned university supervisor views, reviews and grades the academic submissions.

6

Messaging Keeps Users Connected

Students message only their assigned supervisors. Supervisors message assigned students. Coordinators communicate with organisations and invited university supervisors.

Capabilities

Key Platform Features

Everything the department needs to run a structured, transparent, and efficient attachment programme.

🏢

Organisation Selection

Students choose from organisations with available slots. The system blocks placement into full organisations.

👥

Dual Supervisor Model

Every student has one industrial supervisor from the organisation and one university supervisor from UB.

📨

Invite-Based Supervisor Access

Industrial supervisors are invited by organisations. University supervisors are invited by the coordinator using UB email format.

📓

Logbook Review

Students submit weekly logbooks. Assigned university supervisors view and review the entries.

📄

Report Review and Grading

Students submit final reports. Assigned university supervisors review, comment and grade the reports.

💬

Controlled Messaging

Students message assigned supervisors only. Supervisors message assigned students. Coordinators message organisations and invited university supervisors.

🔔

Supervisor Deadlines

Supervisors create deadlines for submittables. Coordinators monitor deadlines but do not create or grade them.

🖼️

Profile Images and Dashboards

Profiles support images, and role dashboards use a clean light theme with UB-inspired background visuals.

Why IAMS

What the Platform Replaces

No More Email Chains

All communication, applications, and submissions happen inside the platform. Nothing gets lost in an inbox or misattributed to the wrong student.

No More Paper Forms

Registration, placement details, supervisor assignment, logbooks, reports and reviews are digital and visible to the relevant role.

No More Spreadsheets

Student lists, placement records, supervisor assignments, messages and deadlines are stored in a structured database for easier tracking.

Complete Placement Visibility

The coordinator can see placement status, organisation capacity, supervisor assignment and communication needs without taking over grading duties.

Structured Evaluation Pipeline

University supervisors review academic submissions while industrial supervisors support workplace progress, keeping responsibilities clear.

One Platform for Everyone

Students, organisations, industrial supervisors, university supervisors, and the coordinator all log into the same system with role-appropriate access.
